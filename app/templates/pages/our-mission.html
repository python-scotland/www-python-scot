Python Scotland's mission is to empower both beginner and expert Pythonistas across Scotland by fostering a supportive and accessible environment for learning and collaboration.

We are dedicated to strengthening the local Python ecosystem by providing support to grassroots initiatives and the local communities within Scotland that are using the Python programming language.

One of our main goals is to be a reliable source of funding and assets for Python communities across Scotland.
